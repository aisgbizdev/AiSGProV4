### User Experience Principles

2. **Visual Hierarchy** - Color-coded zones and levels

**1. Dynamic Branch Management:**
- Add/remove branches
- Assign to PT
- Track employee count per branch

- Define 11 levels
- Set same-level management rules
- Allow level skipping

- Create CEO units
- Assign CBOs to CEO
- Allow CEO direct management to BrM (skip CBO)

- Cannot delete PT/Branch yang ada karyawan aktif
- Cannot delete CEO unit yang ada karyawan
